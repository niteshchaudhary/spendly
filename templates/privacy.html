Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we collect the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information or government-issued identification.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — to display your expense history, generate summaries, and power the features of the app. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting and infrastructure. These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and are not permitted to use your data for any purpose beyond delivering the service. We do not currently integrate with any external financial institutions or data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided in the app. We will respond to all privacy-related enquiries within a reasonable timeframe.
